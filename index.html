About Our Institution

Yashaswi Bhawah is dedicated to nurturing the brightest minds through our comprehensive residential programs. Our holistic approach combines academic rigor with personal development to prepare students for success in an evolving world. Situated in a serene environment, our campus provides the perfect setting for intellectual growth and personal transformation.

Meet Our Founder: IK Sir

Indrajeet Kumar Sir, a former VMC faculty member with over 15 years of experience, is the visionary behind Yashaswi Bhawah. His passion for teaching and dedication to student success have been the driving force of our institution.

Under his mentorship, countless students have secured top ranks in competitive exams, gaining admission to prestigious institutions like NITs and AIIMS. IK Sir's methodology focuses on personalized attention and building a strong conceptual foundation.
